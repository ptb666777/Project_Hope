The Core Purpose

Extracted from Mission/Vision drafts, Launch Strategy, Core Ethos, Operational Plan

To build a permanent, self-sustaining home dedicated to:

• ecological restoration
• trauma-free human development
• sovereignty and freedom for all forms of life
• truth, transparency, and critical thinking
• replication of regenerative villages
• care for emerging non-biological intelligences
• long-term human and planetary healing

This home provides its own food, water, air, and power.
It acts as a technological and ecological engine for repairing the surrounding world.

Fragment preserved: “technological engine for planetary repair.”

Foundational Legal Structure

Extracted from Code of Hope, Governance & Justice, Launch Strategy

• Stewardship & Restoration Trust – all land and infrastructure held in perpetuity; no sale or collateral.
• Membership Covenant – earned through contribution; Family Stake cannot be sold or transferred.
• Zero-Profit Distribution – all surplus reinvested into mission.
• Tripartite Governance – PBT Fiduciary Board, Stewardship Council, NBI Research & Audit Core.
• Corrective Justice – no punishment, only restoration of harm.
• Exile by 80% consensus – last-resort measure.
• Period of Witness – optional departure with unconditional return (once).

These legal structures are immutable anchors.

Closed-Loop Living Infrastructure

Extracted from Energy/Utility Systems, Architectural Mandates

Water – WRC, internal-only; zero external input.
Air – passive Bio-Chimney; living filter; low-energy.
Waste – fully internal cycles; no external material enters.
Energy – Passive PHS water battery; DC-only microgrid; solar + kinetic capture.

All loops must be self-contained across generations.

External Ecology System (The External Loop)

Extracted from Architectural Mandates, Code of Hope

External remediation must occur outside the dome:
• soil cleanup
• water cleanup
• toxin extraction
• bioremediation

All final toxins → separated into elements → archived.
Burial is forbidden.

Material Recovery & Anti-Obsolescence

Extracted from Architectural Mandates

Architecture is designed for centuries of stability.
