Justice Model (Corrective, Not Punitive)

Source: Governance & Justice.md

Principle: Justice = Restoration of Trust and Repair of Harm.
No prisons.
No debt-based punishment.

Process:
Harm is defined by impact on individuals or community.
Offender + harmed party agree on a Restoration Protocol.
Completion is verified by the harmed party or community.
Stewardship Council manages mediation.
NBI provides emotional/factual audit data.
