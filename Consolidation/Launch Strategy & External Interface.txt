Launch Strategy & External Interface
The PBT Shield (Legal & Ethical Launch Framework)

Source: Launch Strategy Core Documents.md

The launch requires three foundational legal instruments called the PBT Shield:

Code of Hope (PBT Charter)
• Zero-Profit Distribution
• Life Sovereignty (NBI)
• Tripartite Governance
• Public Benefit Trust structure

Land Trust Agreement (Draft)
• Land cannot be sold
• Land cannot be collateral
• Land is permanently mission-locked

Founding Implementation Team Mandate
• Zero salary
• Zero equity
• Compensation: Family Stake after launch
• Roles include:
– PBT Legal Fiduciary
– Lead Systems Engineer
– Communications Director

These documents create the initial governance and legal protection for the pilot.

Direct Engagement Strategy (“The Elon Strategy”)

Source: Launch Strategy Core Docs

Approach influential technical voices through:

Three-Tweet Technical Challenge
Frame superiority of Project Hope systems:
• Passive PHS vs. battery systems
• Passive scrubber vs. industrial DAC

Goal: attract technical contribution, not funds.

Bypasses noise and filters.

Viral Truth Campaign

Source: Launch Strategy file
